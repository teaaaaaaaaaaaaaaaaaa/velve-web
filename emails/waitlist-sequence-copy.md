# Velve Waitlist Sequence Copy

Last reviewed: May 16, 2026

## Email 1

When:
immediately after signup

Subject:
`You're on the list for Velve`

Preheader:
`Your waitlist spot is confirmed. We'll email you when access opens.`

Purpose:
confirmation and trust

## Email 2

When:
2-4 days later

Subject:
`What Velve is actually building`

Preheader:
`Closet, discovery, trade, and trust — in one app.`

Primary CTA:
`See what's coming`

Purpose:
brand positioning

Suggested body angle:

- one paragraph on living closets, discovery, and trading
- one paragraph on trust signals: verified profiles, ratings, completed swaps
- one invite to reply to `info@velveapp.com`

## Email 3

When:
10-14 days later

Subject:
`A small update from Velve`

Preheader:
`We're keeping this short: here's what's moving.`

Primary CTA:
`See the latest`

Purpose:
keep the waitlist warm without spamming

Suggested body angle:

- one update only
- one product area only: closet, feed, trades, chat, or virtual try-on
- one CTA

## Email 4

When:
after the audience is warm

Subject:
`Want priority when Velve opens?`

Preheader:
`A priority-access invite for early supporters.`

Primary CTA:
`Unlock Priority`

Purpose:
reward and sharing mechanic

Best reward framing:

- priority access
- founding member status
- early beta for trade/discovery or try-on features

Avoid:

- discount-heavy language
- fake scarcity
- vague "special surprise" hooks

## Email 5

When:
launch wave or beta open

Subject:
`Velve access is opening`

Preheader:
`Your waitlist spot can now turn into access.`

Primary CTA:
`Enter Velve`

Purpose:
conversion

Suggested body angle:

- exact access details
- exact deadline if relevant
- simple CTA, no clutter
